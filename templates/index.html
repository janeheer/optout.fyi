    What are my rights? CCPA / CPRA

    The CCPA/CPRA gives California residents the right to know what personal data companies collect, ask for deletion, and opt out of data sales or sharing. You do not need to be a citizen. If your data was collected in California, these rights apply to you. Companies generally have 45 days to respond.
